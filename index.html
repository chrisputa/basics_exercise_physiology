Basics in Exercise Physiology

Aims and scope

This lecture series addresses the basics of exercise physiology. It is focussed on main principles and aspects of exercise induced effects in humans.

Course Details

Here’s a summary of the key parameters of the Module An Introduction to Sports Medicine and Health Promotion (GSM) that is offered as part of the Bachelor Degree program Bachelor of Science Sportwissenschaft Performance and Health at Friedrich-Schiller-University Jena.

Learning and Qualification Objectives

Understanding of fundamental physiological processes, also in their application to sports; differentiated application of scientific and sports medicine principles to training processes in theory and practice. Development of moderation, communication, and presentation skills in a professional context; development of scientific discourse skills through exchange with others; development of skills for developing and critically evaluating arguments and appreciating different perspectives.
